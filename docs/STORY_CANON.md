# Story Canon: Dystopia / Fractureline

This game is based on Naftali's original DYSTOPIA outline and draft documents.

## Core Premise

The story alternates between two young protagonists living in different eras of the same world.

- Xav Reivax lives in the past, in what appears to be a futuristic utopian society.
- Zelda Adlez lives in the future, after that society has collapsed into dystopia.
- Zelda eventually discovers that she is Xav's grandchild.
- Zelda finds a way to contact Xav in the past to figure out why Cybol's apparent utopia became the future dystopia.
- Xav's timeline should generally remain linear.
- Zelda's timeline can become unstable or jump around because changes in Xav's past alter Zelda's present/future.

## Main Characters

### Xav Reivax

Past-side protagonist.

- Student at the University of Brinkton.
- Lives in the year 874cy.
- Optimistic and somewhat casual.
- Connected to a family object or technology that may enable contact with the future.
- Eventually becomes part of Zelda's family line.

### Yve Ettevy

Past-side supporting character.

- Student in Brinkton in 874cy.
- Xav's close friend/classmate.
- Sharper, more skeptical, and likely more academically focused than Xav.
- Should help ground Xav's everyday life before the future contact becomes undeniable.

### Zelda Adlez

Future-side protagonist.

- Lives on Ayker after the fall of Cybol.
- Lives in a dystopian society in 23ac.
- Finds a way to contact Xav in the past.
- Seeks answers about how the utopia became a dystopia and whether the future can be changed.
- Eventually discovers she is Xav's grandchild.

## Setting

### Ayker

- Small, lush planet.
- Not Earth.
- Gives the story a clean slate for culture, politics, history, and technology.

### Cybol

- Major power on Ayker.
- In Xav's time, Cybol appears advanced, stable, and close to utopian.
- By Zelda's time, Cybol has fallen.
- The cause of Cybol's fall is a central mystery.

### Diderram

- Second major power on Ayker.
- Future history may involve Cybol decimating Diderram, Diderram replacing Cybol, or both depending on timeline changes.
- The relationship between Cybol and Diderram should be one of the core political mysteries.

### Brinkton

- Major city and hub of Cybol society.
- Home of the University of Brinkton.
- Exists as a polished civic/student hub in Xav's time and as ruins/tunnels in Zelda's future.

## Timeline Anchors

### Past

- Year: 874cy.
- Location: University of Brinkton.
- Opening flow should introduce Xav, Yve, Cybol society, and hints that the utopia may not be as simple as advertised.

### Future

- Year: 23ac after the fall of Cybol.
- Location: ruins/tunnels of old Brinkton.
- Zelda discovers or helps uncover a device/object that can contact Xav in the past.

## Existing Draft Beats to Preserve

- Prologue future line: Zelda's side finds something in the ruins of old Brinkton that can change everything.
- Chapter 1 past opening: Xav and Yve meet at the University of Brinkton before or after a Cybol history exam.
- Xav's com/device breaks frequently, which can become an early symptom of cross-time interference.
- A family object/notebook passed down through firstborn family members may be tied to the time communication mechanism.
- Someone not meant to touch the family object may cause dangerous timeline effects.

## Game Structure

The game should use Ink chapter packs.

- Chapter 1 should establish Xav's everyday Cybol life and Zelda's first future contact attempt.
- Chapter 2 should branch based on the Chapter 1 ending/state.
- Future chapter packs should be selected from prior Ink state, not a simple linear chapter number alone.

## No Magic Rule

The original outline leans away from magic.

Use advanced science, time communication, strange devices, records, family artifacts, history manipulation, and sci-fi causality instead of overt magic.

## Thematic Questions

- Did society truly collapse, or did people only discover the cost of the utopia?
- Did Cybol cause the dystopia by trying to preserve perfection?
- Can Zelda change the past without losing her own memories?
- What happens when Xav's actions alter Zelda's timeline?
- Does Zelda remember both timelines, forget the original, or temporarily carry conflicting versions until time settles?
- Is the future's dystopian leader connected to Xav's family line?
